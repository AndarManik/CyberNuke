Nucleotide is a low contrast light theme designed to reduce eye strain in bright environments. The colors used have an approximate contrast ratio of 2 and equal preceived saturation. These choices were made to maximize readability on different screen brightness levels. Ultimately enabling you to code in any bright environment.
